In Disaster Blaster you protect yourself from disasters like tornados and fires which threaten you home, car, and other property. You blast the disasters out of the sky with skillfully aimed shots and some help from IFBI.

  • Touch screen mobile game
  • Different enemy types with different behaviors
  • Wooden block physically/practically built aesthetic
  • Unity with leaderboard integration

Brand New School had produced the original series of animated spots for IFBI based on classic wooden toy automata. The spots were very well received leading Young and Laramore to bring us an interesting challenge: extend the broadcast campaign with a mobile interactive attraction. We took inspiration from early arcade games like Space Invaders and Missile Command and the modern touch-screen interfaces of mobile gaming.

After working closely with Young and Laramore on broad concepts we arrived at creating a mobile game that could be driven to family events around Indiana. The game was installed in the side of a custom, brand-wrapped dune-buggy that toured around Indiana. When visitors finished the game they could enter in their initials to claim their place on the leaderboard. Every player was also entered in daily prize drawing.
