Toys
for Tots

For the 2011 holidays, Brand New School created a series of playful, interactive store-front displays for eBay. Using these displays and their mobile phones, shoppers could purchase and donate toys to Toys for Tots.

  • Interactive display window allowing visitors to donate to Toys 4 Tots through eBay
  • Triggered animations in the display when mobile app activated
  • Objective C, QR codes, touchscreen film

The window display was a virtual toy-store window. Each toy in the window had a QR code gift tag and when shoppers scanned the codes the toy would spring to life and the shopper would be prompted to donate the amount on the tag. Once the donation was complete the whole window would erupt with confetti and a thank you. Children could also interact with the display through a touch screen installed in the window, triggering animations and easter eggs by pressing on-screen buttons.
