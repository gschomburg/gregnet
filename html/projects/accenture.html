Now

The Next News Now interactive display combines body, gesture, and touch tracking to create an engaging experience bringing technology and business news to travelers at Chicago O'Hare International Airport. This project modernized Accenture's existing presence, bringing it up to date with state of the art technology including dual depth-camera based tracking and analytics and high resolution graphics spanning four 4k displays driven from a single app. I was lead developer in the creation of the new installation.

  • Four 4K displays from single app
  • Touchscreen UI
  • Gesture tracking to activate interface before the user even touches
  • Custom built depth camera system for body and gesture tracking
  • Unity, Expression Engine, Kinect Depth Camera

One of the goals of the new design was to attract more users so we started by making a colorful, active design. Then we began looking at ways to make the interactions more engaging. Using computer vision we were able to add a gestural level of interaction into the design. As users approach the wall, it reacts and reveals more layers of content.

The wall contains a curated set of Accenture branded infographics and articles, but the system is open allowing partnerships with other media outlets like Bloomberg. Users are able to browse image galleries and watch recent news reports.
