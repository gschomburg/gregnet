s3iz.com

s3iz is the collaboration of myself and Jason Strand. In 2002 it was our personal and professional portfolios. Much of the work featured on the site was small personal flash projects and experiments, but we also produced some websites for clients. We built a website for an architecture studio and a few photography portfolio sites.

Much of the work has been lost to time, but there is a good archive of our main site and demos here Prepare yourself for some early 2000s grunge, ambient website sounds, tiny text you can't read and flash animation intros. Also looking back, I'm pretty proud we went with a real leet domain name.
