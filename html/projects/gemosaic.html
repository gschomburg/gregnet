GE Mosaic

This installation showcases GE's new branding initiative and provides a fun welcome for visitors to the GE corporate headquarters in Fairfield CT. The installation was designed to showcase their new branding initiative and make something fun for visitors as they entered the building. The installation highlights the four pillars of GE's core services and allows visitors to find out more about the products and research that GE is pioneering.

  • Touchscreen UI
  • Custom built camera system for body tracking
  • Openframeworks, Objective C

The installation was driven by two major interactions; touch via a custom built IR overlay and body tracking provided by a computer vision system. As visitors walked by the installation, the application revealed a mosaic of thumbnails. Touching a thumbnail revealed video content or a slide show featuring GE's latest innovations.
