Brand New
School Website

Brand New School is a design and production studio with offices in NY and LA. As a Senior Interaction Developer at BNS, I was part of the small team that designed and implemented a completely new content management system for their website.

  • Easy for select non-technical staff to update and maintain content
  • Has been used for two significantly different front end re-designs
  • Vimeo, Instagram and Twitter Integration
  • Expression Engine, Javascript, HTML/Sass, Gulp build

In the six years I worked at Brand New School they had ~four different websites. The latest two iterations of the site I had a hand in developing and designing. In the latest iteration we wanted to keep the content fresh while also providing a responsive design for mobile and tablet users. To achieve this, we built a custom CMS using Expression Engine that would allow for quick updates whenever new work was produced. To add a personal touch, we integrated our social media feeds allowing users to see what is happen at the studio in real-time. We also integrated our Vimeo channel into the site. This allowed us to have a cohesive library of content and streamlined our viewing metrics.
