Escape From
Planet Five

Escape from Planet Five is a space-themed VR whack-a-mole game created over three days as part of a Memorial Day weekend hackathon. The hackathon was conceived by myself and Justin Bakse to help us explore Unity VR workflows and experiment with some 3D production tools. We had been making VR prototypes and demos with an Oculus dev kit, but this was the first project we were able to use motion controllers and room scale.

  • VR hackathon game
  • HTC Vive, Unity 3d, Newton VR, Substance Painter

Our goal was to complete a full VR game in three days, so we got jamming. We decided on using a whack-a-mole game mechanic because it was simple, well understood, and wouldn't require us to worry as much about game design. We were able to focus on the VR aspects of gameplay, workflows, and polish.
