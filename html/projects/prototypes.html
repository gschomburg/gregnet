Prototyping

Throughout the course of my career, experimenting with new technology and tools has been one of the most valuable and rewarding experiences. I have gotten to work with a variety of libraries and hardware over the last ten years.

  • Java, Objective C, C#, Actionscript, PHP
  • Unity, Processing, Openframeworks, Meteor
  • Arduino, Kinect, HTC Vive, Oculus Rift

We had several internal projects at Brand New School that allowed us an excuse to work with new tools. We made a BNS app for the iPhone, a web-based studio status board for tracking projects and code repositories, and a web-based image/reference library.

While at TSFIM, we built circuit boards and home-made IR touchscreens. We also built the first version of an IR camera computer vision software which later evolved and was used in several projects. We also built a AIM chatbot that allowed users to enter a MUD version of the studio, complete with crafting and experience points.
