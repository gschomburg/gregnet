Drawbot

The Drawbot is a two-axis whiteboard plotter that Justin Bakse and I prototyped and iterated on in our spare time. Our first prototype was a simple screw drive, built out of zip ties, heat shrink tubing and a yard stick. By our final prototype we were using custom laser cut panels and custom 3d printed parts. The Drawbot is built out of an Arduino with an Adafruit Motorshield and is run by a custom processing app that converts svg graphics into draw commands.
