IBM Color
Billboard

The IBM Supply Chain Billboard was an interactive billboard that changed color based on whatever was in front of it.

  • DMX controlled architectural LEDs
  • Custom fabricated signage
  • Processing, custom computer vision system

The sign was custom fabricated to our specifications and contained 16 MIDI controllable high power LED arrays. The color detection software used a camera feed to sample colors in front of the sign and the lights were calibrated to best represent the sampled color.

The sign was installed in JFK Airport for several weeks sampling and recording colors. After the installation, the sign was packed up and last I heard it was on it's way to Epcot Center to be placed into storage. I like to imagine that it's there next to the Arc of the Covenant.
