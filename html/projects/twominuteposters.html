Two Minute
Posters

Two Minute Posters was an art project designed to boost my ideation process and composition skills. In the project, I was exploring the temporal relationship between creation and engagement. My primary focus was the amount of time a designer might spend creating an advertisement or poster relative to the amount of time a single person might spend looking at it.

With that relationship in mind I set about creating 1200 posters. I spent only two minutes creating each one. Each poster is shown back-to-back, for only a tenth of a second, resulting in a 2 minute video.
