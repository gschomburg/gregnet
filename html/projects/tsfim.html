In 2007, I moved to NYC to work with Justin Bakse and Eric Eckhardt at The Studio for Interactive Media. There I designed and developed interactive installations and media for a wide range of clients. We did projects for major corporations such as Adobe, Coca-Cola, Sony, IBM and for museums and nonprofits. We built websites, kiosks, circuit prototypes, installations and computer vision software.
