Sketching
in Hardware

Sketching in Hardware is an annual creative conference focused on physical computing, the Internet of Things, and rapid prototyping. For the last seven years, I have designed and developed the identities, websites, and t-shirts for each conference, reflecting that year's location and theme.

For each new identity and website I try to integrate either some generative design or new technology. One year, I 3D printed some custom letterpress type. Another year, I created a Processing app to create isometric maze designs. In addition, I have also been designing a new single-stroke font for each conference.
