License to
Dream

The Brizo License to Dream interactive installation was a touch-activated digital sign installed at the Kitchen and Bath Industry Show in Chicago.

  • Integrated with touch faucet circuit
  • Display built into glass bottom sink
  • Custom circuit, Objective C

The installation was created at The Studio for Interactive Media with Brand New School. BNS had already created a series of whimsical broadcast spots which Brizo wanted to translate into an interactive installation showcasing their new touch faucets.

The faucet sink had a display mounted in the bottom that came to life when the faucet was turned on. As the sink animation played out a giant LED screen behind the faucet also would play a mix of hand drawn and randomly generated animations. We designed and built a custom interface board that integrated the touch-faucet control with our media display.
