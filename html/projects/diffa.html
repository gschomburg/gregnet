Dining
By Design

While at the Studio for Interactive Media we designed and fabricated this custom LED lighting element for the GE Monogram table for the 2007 DIFFA Dining by Design event.

  • Hand fabricated strands of LEDs
  • Dynamically controlled lighting system

One hundred and thirty LEDs twinkle on and off below the mirrored ceiling. I fabricated each strand by hand, wrapping wire around each LED. In the end we created a hybrid of barbed wire and christmas tree lights.
