Center for
Total Health

The Kaiser Permanente Center For Total Health is an exhibition and meeting space in Washington D.C. Brand New School created a suite of interactive exhibits for the launch of the center. It was a massive project that included producing a mini documentary, six dual screen interactive applications and an 80-foot-long touch-screen application.

  • 80-foot touch application
  • Suite of touch screen applications
  • Cinder, Unity, Vuforia AR, HTML

Each of the exhibits examined a different health topic or challenge and each had a unique interface.To help unify the different apps and increase accessibility an alternate UI was designed that spanned all of the apps and allowed more direct access of content. Our work with Kaiser Permanente also included the website for the center and an augmented reality app that showcased KP's innovate exam-room design.
