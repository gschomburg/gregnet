Neocon

NeoCon is a design exposition and conference for commercial interiors that takes place in Chicago each year. In 2014, working in a small team, I helped design and build the software and hardware powering a special exhibit for the Herman Miller showroom at the conference. The exhibit showcased several animated sequences illustrating Herman Miller's design philosophy and process. A custom-fabricated table housed both the screens and physical switches used to control the animations.

  • Installation
  • Custom hardware chassis fabrication
  • Unique hardware physical interface
  • Arduino, Processing

We worked with Herman Miller to prototype and design the experience. We coordinated with the table fabricator to determine technical specs needed to accommodate screens and controls. We worked with the animation studio to ensure that the animation and experience would work together seamlessly.
