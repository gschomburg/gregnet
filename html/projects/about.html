About

Hi, my name is Greg. I am a creative coder with a background in art and design. I have been designing and developing interactive experiences and installations for the past fifteen years. Over the course of my career, I've had the opportunity to work with a wide range of technologies, languages, and frameworks. My current focus is Unity and C# development for virtual and augmented reality experiences. I am currently looking for interesting projects where I can best utilize my talents.
