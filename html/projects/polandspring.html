Raising
Spirit

Poland Spring has had a presence at mile 19 of the Boston Marathon for years. In 2014 I helped create a road-side installation during the marathon designed to boost community spirit and help raise money for the One Fund Boston, a relief organization benefiting people affected by the 2013 Boston Marathon bombing.

  • Live event production and show control
  • Realtime audio input and visualizer
  • Multiple integrated live HD video feeds
  • Unity with mobile integration

The installation consisted of two massive 15 foot wide video billboards positioned on either side of the street. The billboards were fully audio-reactive meters that would react to the cheering marathon crowd. As people cheered the billboard would visualize their volume and intensity. Every time the crowd filled the meter, Poland spring would donate to the One Fund. By the end of the marathon the $10,000 goal had been met.

The billboard application was built as separate modules that could be activated live during the event. Some of the modules contained brand messaging, some displayed live video feeds overlaid with motivational messages for the racers. Show organizers could switch between three live feeds and activate specific branding modules using control software on their phones.
